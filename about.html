Ved Garbh Sanskar provides a platform for best progeny to couples, offering authentic Ayurvedic antenatal care and pregnancy activities all in one place under the humble guidance of Ayurvedic doctors.

Complete Ayurvedic & Garbh Sanskar Center

We are Ahmedabad's premier destination for both authentic Ayurvedic treatments and comprehensive Garbh Sanskar. Our clinic provides natural healing for various ailments under the expert guidance of seasoned Ayurvedic doctors.

For expecting parents, we provide a specialized platform for the best progeny, featuring pregnancy activities like Yog, Pranayam, Meditation with Garbh Samvad, Music, a dedicated library, and Garbh ki Pathshala.

Meet Our Expert

Dr. Daxa M Patel

B.A.M.S., Gujarat Ayurved University (1997)

Dr. Daxa M Patel is a highly respected Ayurvedic practitioner with over 26 years of clinical experience. After graduating from the prestigious Gulabkuverba Ayurvedic College, Jamnagar, she has dedicated her life to holistic healing.

She specializes in authentic Ayurvedic antenatal care and is the visionary behind Ahmedabad's first unique Garbh Sanskar center. Her approach seamlessly blends ancient wisdom with modern lifestyle adjustments to empower families on their journey to parenthood.

Her deep understanding of Garbh Samvad, Ayurvedic dietetics, and spiritual rituals has guided thousands of couples toward delivering healthy, conscious, and vibrant children.
