The Science of Garbh Sanskar

Garbh Sanskar translates to "education in the womb." It is a profound Ayurvedic science that focuses on the mental, physical, and spiritual development of the child during pregnancy.

Our program involves a curated blend of specific diets, soothing music (Garbh Sangeet), yoga, positive thinking, and spiritual chanting designed to create an optimal environment for your baby's growth.
